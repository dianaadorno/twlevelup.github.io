About LevelUp

LevelUp is a series of classes, and conferences for students who want to add to their classroom learning with real-world experience. Topics range from test-driven development, continuous delivery and pair programming to Agile project management, business analysis and how employers hire grads.

LevelUp EXP conferences are single day events. LevelUp courses run 6-9 weeks. LevelUp is a non-for-profit initiative, run by ThoughtWorks volunteers.
